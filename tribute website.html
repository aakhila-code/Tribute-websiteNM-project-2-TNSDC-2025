Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was the leader of India's non-violent independence movement against British rule. He is remembered for his philosophy of nonviolence and truth, which inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

Gandhi led India to independence through peaceful protests, emphasizing the power of self-reliance, simplicity, and unity.

"You must be the change you wish to see in the world." - Mahatma Gandhi
